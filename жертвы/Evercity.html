EVERCITY ECOSYSTEM

WHAT IS EVERCITY ECOSYSTEM?

We are Security token accelerator, VC fund and Smart sustainable city (SSC) hubs network governed by DAO principles. Our mission is to bridge breakthrough solutions with venture and crypto capital.

WHAT PROBLEM DO WE SOLVE?

  • The existing solutions to world’s urbanization problems are fragmented and hard to implement.
  • Million-dollar ICO scams make it hard for investors to choose reliable and promising tech startups.

WHAT IS THE SOLUTION?

Evercity finds best startups and prepares them for investment and implementation in smart cities across the globe through Accelerator, VC and SSC Hub ecosystem.
